Gravitas Events


Lets EMPOWER !!


graVITas, the Techno-Management Extravaganza organized by VIT, Vellore is an internationally acclaimed technical fest. It aims in exposing the budding graduates to recent trends in various domains, challenging the young brains to solve technical problems whilst interacting with their peers. Amongst the plethora of events held, FEPSI is proud to present to you our four technical events in this fest, namely Pitch Perfect, Socio-White Hack, Startathon, and Smart India transformation. Gravitas is a fest that occurs during October, featuring a multitude of events for students within and outside of VIT to participate in.

Pitch Perfect
Do you have the knack to think out of the box?

Something you sense separates you from the rest? Want to solve first world problems with the luxury of being rewarded for your efforts? We're here to tell you that we've got the perfect platform for you.

FEPSI is back with the third edition of Pitch Perfect and we're excited to have you join us.

Socio White Hack
IT industries plan to revolutionize the cyber world by bringing in the right people with proficient technical knowledge within these domains.

Come join us for the Socio-white Hack where we'll teach you everything you need to know including a hands-on session from a well renowned industry expert to hone your skills for what is to come.
