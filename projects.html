Our Projects


FEPSI is not just working as co-member


Through three different projects Ullas, GLL and Soch which are present we try to inculcate all the qualities of a responsible human being who is confident and has values to contribute to the society.

With a mere 6 years since its inception, FEPSI has earned a lot of respect for the work it does. The sincerity and the dedication of its members towards the theme of the club has been its impetus all along.

ULLAS

Ullas is the face of our NGO, FEPSI. Therefore, when the events held by this project are successful, the whole crew gets off on the right foot. Holding outreaches which are essentially the cornerstone of this NGO has ensured that we uphold our goal which is to help those in need with all our heart. As the years have passed,we have done nothing but find ways to ameliorate the experiences we provide to those occupying orphanages.

SOCH

The term “Soch” literally translates to “a thought”, which is one of the first steps to a revolution. All major changes and progressions in the world start with “a thought”. All leaders become great with “a thought”.

GLL

GLL means global leadership labs. This project aims in inculcating and improving leadership qualities present in oneself. It is conducted as a semester long project. Various activities are involved under GLL which slowly, with time, help the participants to grow from within and recognize their strengths and weaknesses. Moreover, in this small and simple process of learning, all of us will overcome our weakest areas, become motivated and be much more confident about our goals.

ULLAS

Birthday Campaign

Celebrations are always sweeter when shared. You are someone who has the ability to become a beacon of hope to others. This year, on your Birthday, be the change you want to see. As a Birthday Gift, give yourself the ability to do something great. Set up a fundraiser to change somebody’s life for the better. Restore their sense of pride, self-sufficiency, and independence. We all have the luxury and money to spend it on cakes, gifts, clothes, shoes, and whatnot. But remember, there are millions of people who don't have more than one pair of shoes or clothes, don't have a house to live in, they also do not possess enough money to celebrate their birthday. So we, the members of FEPSI came up with the BIRTHDAY CAMPAIGN which is an initiative to celebrate our birthday with children, celebrate our happiness with theirs, and donate whatever you feel like- such as food, clothes, books, money to get a smile on their face. We celebrate our birthday with the children and conduct a donation drive and collect whatever we can books, clothes, pencils, crayons, money, and donate it to the children.

Project Harita

An initiative called Haritha was birthed with the sole purpose of giving back to mother nature.We have incorporated this initiative in our outreaches as well. The first time we conducted Haritha was during our outreach to Sacred Heart Boarding School. To call it a success would be an understatement. Voluntary contributions are taken from club members and a few others by whom saplings are planted while in outreaches.This helps accomplish our goal to replenish mother nature’s rich reserves.

Library Campaign

We all agree that every child deserves equal opportunities in life and there is no better way of doing this than by giving them the power of knowledge. So we at FEPSI with this basic ideology, have come up with an initiative "Library Campaign", where we are collecting English and Tamil books for primary and secondary school students to set up an entire library in their boarding school. We request you to donate your old books so that your little contribution can become someone's bundle of joy.

Mannat

Mannat is an initiative in which clothes are collected from our happiness stalls set up during Riviera, FEPSI’s logistics, and external sources as well. It was made sure that the clothes were provided to the needy and old people that can’t earn, and not to people who are capable of earning. Our mission is to improve the quality of life for the shelter-less to break the cycle of poverty. This event has brought smiles to the ones in need and to the volunteers as well.

Akshaya

The food donation drives held my Project Ullas allow us to do our part for the people around us by reducing their plight of hunger because we in FEPSI believe that one of the ways to touch a person’s soul is through food. For most of us, food is easy to come by but the same cannot be said for all of the people around us. Doing our part to feed those in need is an ultimate form of service to humanity as a full stomach brings peace to both the body and the mind. Akshaya is a series of food contributions initiated by Ullas. The objective of these food donation drives is to distribute food as a group or individually to the people who have very little access to proper nutrition. Sometimes we also join hands with local trusts and charities in order to get the maximum reach to the people in need. The core belief that we as Fepsians embody is to help and contribute as much as we can, wherever we are because “no one has ever become poor by giving”-Anne Frank.

Curriculum

The GLL curriculum covers several events and experiences ranging from fun-filled activities to educational ones which help develop all the skills a leader should possesses, within a span of two semesters. Each event is an enriching experience which helps the participants to learn how to work in a team, improvise and practice their public speaking skills and work on their creativity. The curriculum not only shapes the leaders of tomorrow but also plays a huge role in developing a vibrant and inquisitive society.
